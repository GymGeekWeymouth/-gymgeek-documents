Potential Hazard Who Is at Risk Existing Control Measures Risk Rating Preventative Measures Required Date Completed
Opening and Closing Premises Alone Staff Lone working policy communicated at induction. Staff notify a director before opening or closing alone. Check-in call or message within 30 minutes of arrival and at close. Entry and exit times logged in lone working register. Premises alarm system operational — staff trained in use. ✓ Low Missed check-in: designated contact attempts to reach staff within 15 minutes. Escalation procedure in place. Register reviewed monthly by directors.
Delivering One-to-One Sessions Alone Staff, Members 1:1 sessions by lone staff permitted with prior director agreement only. Session time, client name and expected end time logged before session begins. Mobile phone accessible throughout. Gym door remains unlocked during all client-facing sessions. Client PAR-Q on file before any 1:1. ✓ Low Directors informed of any 1:1 session conducted by lone worker. Staff to contact director if client presents with unexpected health concerns.
Working Alone Outside Normal Hours Staff Out-of-hours lone working pre-approved by a director. Staff sign in and out on lone working register. Check-in required every 2 hours. Building security systems active. Emergency contact list posted in staff area. ✓ Low No lone working out of hours without director knowledge. Director to attend site or contact emergency services if staff member fails to check in.
Verbal Aggression or Threatening Behaviour Staff All staff trained in de-escalation and conflict awareness. Staff never to engage with or restrain an aggressive individual. Staff to leave the situation and call for assistance or emergency services if threatened. All incidents logged. Repeat or serious incidents reported to police. ▲ Med Zero tolerance policy communicated to all members. All incidents reported to a director same day. Lone working policy reviewed following any incident.
Medical Emergency While Working Alone Staff, Members At least one staff member with current first aid qualification on site at all times. First aid kit and AED accessible — locations clearly displayed. Emergency services number and full site address posted in gym and staff area. Staff to call 999 immediately for serious medical emergency. ✓ Low Lone workers must not leave a casualty unattended unless necessary to call for help. Directors notified of any medical emergency immediately. First aid kit restocked following any use.
Slip, Trip or Injury to Lone Worker Staff Lone workers maintain tidy and clear working environment. Mobile phone kept on person at all times. Staff to inform a director of any injury, however minor, sustained while lone working. First aid kit accessible throughout premises. ✓ Low Lone workers to call 999 if seriously injured and unable to self-treat. Check-in procedure ensures regular contactability. Incident report completed for all injuries.
