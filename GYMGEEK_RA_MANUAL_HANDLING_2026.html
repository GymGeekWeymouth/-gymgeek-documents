Potential Hazard Who Is at Risk Existing Control Measures Risk Rating Preventative Measures Required Date Completed
Lifting Heavy Gym Equipment Staff Manual handling training at induction. Load assessed before lifting — weight, shape, grip, distance. Assistance or mechanical aids for loads over 20kg. Equipment stored at waist height where possible. Adequate space maintained around storage areas. ✓ Low Staff to report difficulty or pain when lifting immediately. Mechanical aids used for heavy loads. Annual technique refresher.
Moving Rowers on/off Wall Brackets Staff Rowers moved by staff only — notices on wall. Two staff required when moving from brackets. Proper grip and technique — knees bent, back straight. Floor area clear before moving. Staff trained on correct rower handling at induction. ✓ Low Members must not attempt to move rowers. Staff to assess area before moving equipment.
Carrying Force Plates and Assessment Equipment Staff Force plates carried by two staff where practicable. Stored at floor or waist level. No overhead lifting. Staff to wear appropriate footwear. ✓ Low Plan route before carrying. Trolley recommended for longer distances. Report damage to a director.
Moving Office Furniture and Fixtures Staff Manual handling principles applied to all lifting. Staff assess load before moving. Assistance sought for awkward or heavy items. Boxes stored at accessible heights. ✓ Low Avoid twisting when carrying loads. Break down heavy loads into smaller portions where possible.
Repetitive Handling During Session Setup Staff Equipment laid out in advance to reduce rushed lifting. Equipment stored near point of use. Staff encouraged to take short breaks between repeated lifting tasks. Adequate setup time in timetable. ✓ Low Staff to report fatigue or muscle soreness. Task rotation used where multiple staff available.
Member Self-Service Weights and Equipment Members All members inducted on safe equipment handling. Weights labelled with values. Racks organised lightest to heaviest from floor level upward. Members advised to use a spotter for heavy lifts. Staff available on floor to advise on technique. ✓ Low Members reminded at induction not to attempt loads beyond their capability. Staff to intervene if unsafe technique observed.
